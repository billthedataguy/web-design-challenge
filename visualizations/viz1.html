Vizualization 1

This plot visualizes how temperature is related to distance from the equator (i.e., latitude) for a random sample of approximately 500 world cities. It appears to show - as we would expect - that temperatures go up as you get nearer to the equator (latitude = 0), and go down as you get further from the equator.
